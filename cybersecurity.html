Cybersecurity

Primary Cybersecurity measures include:

  • Access control: Implementing mechanisms to authenticate and authorize users and devices to access resources.
  • Encryption: Encoding data to prevent unauthorized access and maintain its confidentiality.
  • Firewalls and intrusion detection/prevention systems: Deploying barriers and monitoring tools to detect and block malicious activities.
  • Security policies and procedures: Establishing guidelines and protocols for employees and users to follow to mitigate risks.
  • Vulnerability management: Identifying, prioritizing, and addressing weaknesses in systems and software.
  • Incident response: Developing strategies and plans to manage and recover from security breaches or incidents effectively.
  • Security awareness training: Educating employees and users about cybersecurity best practices and threats.
